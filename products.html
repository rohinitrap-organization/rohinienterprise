Round Grow Bags
Cylindrical Fabric Pots · Air-Pruning

Cylindrical non-woven HDPE fabric pots. The breathable fabric promotes air-pruning of the root tip when it reaches the bag wall — the tip dehydrates and dies back, stimulating the plant to grow a denser, more fibrous root system. This results in faster growth and higher yields compared to traditional clay or plastic pots. Excess heat escapes from all sides, keeping roots cool.

Reinforced rim prevents the bag from collapsing when filled. Free-draining base eliminates waterlogging. Compact, dense packing in container significantly reduces freight cost per unit vs clay pots. Folds flat for efficient storage when empty. Available in retail packs (4, 5 or 10 pcs per pack) for garden centre display.

Azolla Cultivation Beds
Multilayer HDPE Geomembrane · UV-Stabilised

Compact, cost-effective cultivation troughs manufactured from multilayered HDPE reinforced geomembrane for azolla (Azollaceae) biofertiliser and livestock feed production. Azolla is a floating aquatic fern hosting symbiotic blue-green algae (Anabaena azollae) that fixes atmospheric nitrogen — making it both a natural fertiliser and a high-protein (25–35%) livestock supplement at very low cost.

The growing decline in traditional fodder availability across South Asia, East Africa and GCC is driving demand for azolla as a sustainable dairy feed supplement. GRIL Azolla beds install above the ground — no digging or pit excavation required. Several beds can be erected within hours. UV-stabilised, puncture-resistant, fungus-resistant and waterproof. Optional shade-net cover integration.

Vermibeds
450 GSM Multilayer HDPE · BIS Standard

Vermicomposting converts organic waste into rich vermicompost through earthworm activity. Earthworms consume decomposed material and excrete it as vermicast — one of the most nutrient-dense organic fertilisers available. Vermibeds are the future of organic farming, converting all organic waste into nourishing crop manure entirely naturally through earthworm activity.

Rohini Enterprise vermibeds are manufactured from 450 GSM multilayer coated HDPE fabric — UV-resistant, puncture-resistant, waterproof and unaffected by most chemicals for long-term durability. Built-in aeration mesh windows maintain optimal moisture and oxygen levels inside the bed. Vermiwash outlet at base corner collects the liquid organic pesticide/fertiliser produced during composting.
