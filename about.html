The Factory

Built on one floor.
Not across three suppliers.

Rohini Enterprises sits in Ahmedabad — inside India's densest belt for HDPE extrusion and technical textile weaving. Resin in, tested goods out. Every product line — tarpaulins, grow bags, azolla beds, vermibeds and nursery bags — runs off the same floor. One PO, one inspection, one set of export documents for a mixed container.

Export documentation, sample swatches and lab reports are prepared as part of production — not bolted on afterward. That is the difference between a supplier and a factory that exports.
